Privacy Policy

Your ideas stay yours.

Effective July 23, 2026

Relay is designed to help people discover and keep useful ideas. This policy explains how Relay handles information, including the optional private cloud sync available in Relay 1.1.

Local use

You can use Relay without turning on cloud sync. Notes and saved content are stored on your device first. Relay does not send your private notes to its cloud service unless you choose to turn on Private cloud sync.

Optional private cloud sync

If you turn on Private cloud sync, Relay asks you to continue with Apple and creates a private Relay cloud account. Relay receives a stable account identifier from Apple so it can restore your account across your devices. Relay does not request your name, email address, or Apple password.

Relay sends the private content you choose to sync—such as questions, answers, topics, and follow-up questions—to your private cloud account. This content is not made public or shared with other Relay users by the current sync feature.

Service and security information

When you use Private cloud sync, our cloud provider may process your IP address, device or app request information, and authentication events such as sign-in, sign-out, and token refresh. We use this information only to operate the service, prevent abuse, troubleshoot problems, and protect account security.

Cloud provider and location

Relay uses Supabase to provide authentication, database, and synchronization services. Relay's primary Supabase project is deployed in the Canada Central region. Supabase and its infrastructure providers may process limited operational and security information as needed to provide the service.

Retention and deletion

Your private cloud content is kept while your Relay cloud account remains active. You can delete the account and its private cloud content in Relay by going to Library → Private cloud sync → Manage → Erase cloud copy. This keeps the notes already stored on your device. Limited security logs and backup copies may remain temporarily until they expire through the cloud provider's ordinary retention cycle.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

Relay does not use third-party advertising SDKs or track you across apps or websites. We do not sell personal information. The operational information described above is used only for app functionality, reliability, abuse prevention, and security.

Information you send us

If you contact support by email, we receive the information you choose to include, such as your email address and message. We use it only to respond, troubleshoot, and improve Relay.

Children's privacy

Relay is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

Relay will evolve. If our data practices change, we will update this policy and its effective date before those changes apply.
