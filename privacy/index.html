Privacy Policy

Your ideas stay yours.

Effective July 14, 2026

Relay is designed to help people discover and keep useful ideas. This policy describes how the current Relay 1.0 app handles information.

Information we collect

Relay 1.0 does not require an account and does not collect personal information through the app. The sample content included in the app is stored locally as part of the application experience.

Analytics and tracking

Relay 1.0 does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking. We do not sell personal information.

Information you send us

If you contact support by email, we receive the information you choose to include, such as your email address and message. We use it only to respond, troubleshoot, and improve Relay.

Children's privacy

Relay is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

Relay will evolve. If our data practices change, we will update this policy and its effective date before those changes apply.

Contact
